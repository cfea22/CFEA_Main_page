Welcome to Our Educational Community

The Career Foundation Educational Academy is a premier educational trust committed to providing quality education in Manipur. Established with a vision to create transformative learning experiences, we operate two prestigious institutions under our umbrella:

  • Career Foundation Academy (CFA): Our school is committed to delivering excellence in education, nurturing young minds from Pre-Nursery to Class X with a holistic approach that balances academics, creativity, and character development to prepare students for a bright future.
  • Career Foundation English Academy (CFEA): Our vocational training center focuses on skill development programs that make students industry-ready with practical, job-oriented training in various professional fields.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies, we are shaping the future of education in Northeast India. Our registered trust status ensures transparency and commitment to our educational mission.
